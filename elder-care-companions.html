Elder Care Services At Home

As people age, the need for companionship and assistance with daily tasks increases. Track Home Care offers compassionate and experienced elder care companions who provide more than just physical support. Our companions are trained to engage with seniors, offering emotional support, companionship, and assistance with day-to-day activities. Whether it's helping with mobility, medication reminders, meal preparation, or simply providing a friendly face, our companions ensure that seniors maintain their dignity, independence, and quality of life.

We take pride in offering companionship that enhances the mental and emotional well-being of elderly individuals. Our companions are carefully selected based on their experience, empathy, and ability to connect with clients. We aim to create a safe and enriching environment where your loved ones feel cared for and respected.

Our Different Kinds of Elder Home Nursing Care Services Delhi, India

  • Personal Care Assistance : Help with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and feeding to ensure hygiene and dignity.
  • Companionship Services : Providing emotional support and companionship to prevent loneliness, offering conversation, reading, and recreational activities.
  • Medication Management : Ensuring that elderly individuals take their prescribed medications on time, along with monitoring for any side effects or reactions.
  • Mobility Assistance : Support with walking, transferring from bed to chair, and using mobility aids, to improve independence and reduce the risk of falls.
  • Chronic Disease Management : Monitoring and managing chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, or arthritis, including regular vital checks and medication administration.
  • Nursing Care : Specialized care for elderly individuals with more complex medical needs, such as wound care, injections, or intravenous therapy, provided by trained nurses.
  • Alzheimerâ€™s and Dementia Care : Specialized support for elderly patients suffering from cognitive disorders, focusing on memory assistance, managing confusion, and ensuring safety.
  • Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation : Home-based physiotherapy to help elderly individuals recover from injuries, surgeries, or strokes, and improve mobility and flexibility.
  • Post-Hospitalization Care : Care following discharge from the hospital, including monitoring recovery, administering medication, and helping with daily activities.
  • Palliative and End-of-Life Care : Comfort care for elderly individuals in the advanced stages of illness, focusing on pain relief, emotional support, and quality of life.
