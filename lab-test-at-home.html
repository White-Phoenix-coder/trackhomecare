Lab Test Services At Home

Track Home Care also offers lab test services to patients who are unable to visit a medical facility for diagnostic testing. We provide home-based laboratory services that allow patients to undergo blood tests, urine tests, and other diagnostic procedures in the comfort of their own home. Our trained professionals collect samples, ensuring accuracy and proper handling before sending them to a laboratory for analysis.

This service is particularly beneficial for individuals with mobility issues, chronic conditions requiring regular testing, or those recovering from surgery who find it difficult to travel. By offering home lab tests, Track Home Care eliminates the need for patients to leave their homes, making medical testing more convenient and accessible.

What are the different kinds of home nursing care services

Lab test services at home in Delhi, India, offer patients the convenience of getting diagnostic tests done in the comfort of their home. These services are particularly beneficial for elderly individuals, bedridden patients, or those with mobility issues. Here are the different types of lab test services at home:

  • Blood Tests
    Common Tests : Complete Blood Count (CBC), Lipid Profile, Liver Function Test (LFT), Kidney Function Test (KFT), Thyroid Profile, Blood Glucose, and more.
    Service : Home collection of blood samples by trained phlebotomists, with test results delivered electronically or physically.
  • Urine Tests
    Common Tests : Urine Routine, Urine Culture, Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) tests, and Kidney function tests.
    Service : Sample collection from home with reports sent directly to the patient.
  • ECG (Electrocardiogram)
    Purpose : To monitor heart health, diagnose arrhythmias, or assess heart disease risks.
    Service : Mobile ECG machines can be brought to your home for quick monitoring of heart activity.
  • X-Ray Services
    Purpose : Diagnostic imaging for conditions like fractures, pneumonia, or chest infections.
    Service : Some companies offer home-based X-ray services where the X-ray technician visits the home with portable X-ray machines.
  • Pregnancy Tests
    Purpose : To detect early signs of pregnancy through blood or urine testing.
    Service : Blood or urine samples can be collected at home for pregnancy-related tests.
  • COVID-19 Tests
    Tests Offered : RT-PCR tests, Rapid Antigen Tests, and Antibody Tests.
    Service : Sample collection for COVID-19 testing at home, with results usually available within a few hours to a few days.
  • Pathology Tests
    Common Tests :
    Tests for cancer markers, vitamin deficiencies, hormone levels, and genetic tests. Service : In-home sample collection for specialized pathology tests, with lab results sent to the patient.
  • Diabetes Tests
    Tests Offered : Blood Glucose, HbA1c, Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT), and other tests related to diabetes.
    Service : Home-based collection of blood samples for diabetes testing and continuous monitoring services.
  • Cholesterol and Lipid Profile Tests
    Purpose : To measure cholesterol and lipid levels to assess heart disease risks.
    Service : Home collection of blood samples for lipid profile and cholesterol testing, with results provided digitally.
  • Hormone Testing
    Common Tests : Thyroid Function Tests (T3, T4, TSH), Testosterone, Estrogen, Progesterone, and others.
    Service : Blood sample collection from home to test hormone levels, often important for conditions related to metabolism, fertility, or menopause.
  • Vitamin and Mineral Deficiency Tests
    Common Tests : Vitamin D, B12, Calcium, Iron, and other essential micronutrient levels.
    Service : Blood sample collection at home, with detailed reports on deficiencies that may be causing fatigue or other health issues.
  • Liver and Kidney Function Tests
    Tests Offered : Liver Function Tests (LFT), Kidney Function Tests (KFT), and other organ function assessments.
    Service : Blood sample collection at home for assessing the health of liver and kidney functions.
  • Allergy Testing
    Purpose : Identifying allergens causing allergic reactions, such as pollen, dust, or certain foods.
    Service : Blood sample collection for allergy tests to determine sensitivities or triggers.
  • Thyroid Function Tests
    Common Tests : TSH, Free T3, and Free T4.
    Service : Home collection of blood samples for thyroid-related tests to diagnose conditions like hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism.
  • Stool and Fecal Tests
    Common Tests : Stool Culture, Occult Blood Test, Parasite Check, etc.
    Service : Collection of stool samples at home for tests related to gastrointestinal health.
  • Spirometry (Lung Function Test)
    Purpose : To assess lung health, often for patients with conditions like asthma, COPD, or other respiratory disorders.
    Service : Home visits by trained technicians to perform lung function tests using portable spirometers.
  • Blood Coagulation Tests
    Tests Offered : Prothrombin Time (PT), International Normalized Ratio (INR), Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time (aPTT), etc.
    Service : Blood sample collection at home for coagulation tests to monitor bleeding disorders or anticoagulant therapy.
  • Food Intolerance Testing
    Purpose : Identifying food intolerances or sensitivities to gluten, lactose, and other foods.
    Service : Home sample collection for lab tests to identify possible food allergies or intolerances.
  • Cancer Screening Tests
    Tests Offered : Screening for common cancers such as breast cancer (mammography), prostate cancer (PSA test), and others.
    Service : Home-based consultations for necessary samples, such as blood tests or swabs, for early detection of cancers.
  • Sleep Apnea Testing
    Purpose : To diagnose sleep disorders, especially sleep apnea, through overnight monitoring.
    Service : Home-based sleep study devices for monitoring breathing patterns and sleep stages.
  • Genetic Testing
    Purpose : To detect genetic disorders or assess risks for conditions like cancer, heart disease, and more.
    Service : Collection of saliva or blood samples at home for genetic screening.
