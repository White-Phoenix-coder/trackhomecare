What are the different kinds of home nursing care services

In Delhi, India, home healthcare services often include the purchase or rental of medical equipment for patients who require ongoing care or specialized treatment at home. These services offer a wide range of equipment, depending on the patient's condition and healthcare needs. Below are different types of purchase or rent equipment services at home:

  1. Oxygen Concentrators and Cylinders
    Rent/Buy: Oxygen concentrators or cylinders are rented or purchased for patients with respiratory issues like COPD, asthma, pneumonia, or post-COVID recovery. Portable and stationary options available for home use.
  2. Ventilators
    Rent/Buy : Ventilators are essential for patients with severe respiratory failure or those recovering from surgeries requiring prolonged ventilation support. Invasive and non-invasive options depending on the severity of the condition.
  3. CPAP/BiPAP Machines
    Rent/Buy : Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) or Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) machines are used for managing obstructive sleep apnea and other respiratory disorders.
    Rental options for short-term use or to try before buying.
  4. Wheelchairs and Walkers
    Rent/Buy : Wheelchairs, motorized wheelchairs, and walkers are available for patients with mobility issues, post-surgery recovery, or chronic conditions like arthritis.
    Manual and powered options are offered based on the patient's needs.
  5. Hospital Beds
    Rent/Buy : Adjustable hospital beds with features like elevation, side rails, and easy positioning are provided for patients needing prolonged care at home.
    Electric and manual beds available for rental or purchase.
  6. Infusion Pumps
    Rent/Buy : Infusion pumps are used for controlled delivery of fluids, medications, or nutrients. They are essential for patients undergoing chemotherapy, long-term antibiotics, or post-surgery recovery.
    Portable infusion pumps for home use are also available.
  7. Nebulizers
    Rent/Buy : Nebulizers are used to deliver medications directly to the lungs for respiratory conditions like asthma or chronic bronchitis.
    Portable and home-use nebulizers can be rented or purchased.
  8. Patient Lifts
    Rent/Buy : Patient lifts assist in transferring patients with limited mobility from beds to chairs, showers, or other areas safely.
    Hydraulic or electric lifts for home use can be rented or bought.
  9. Pressure Relief Mattresses
    Rent/Buy : These specialized mattresses prevent bedsores for patients who are bedridden for extended periods.
    Air mattresses or foam mattresses can be rented or bought based on the patient’s needs.
  10. ECG Machines
    Rent/Buy : Portable ECG machines are available for home monitoring of patients with heart conditions. These can be used by healthcare providers or at-home caregivers.
    Compact and easy-to-use devices can be rented for short-term monitoring or purchased for long-term use.
  11. Blood Pressure Monitors
    Rent/Buy : Automatic or manual blood pressure monitors are available for regular monitoring of patients with hypertension or other heart-related issues.
    Digital and wrist monitors are popular for home care settings.
  12. Suction Machines
    Rent/Buy : Suction machines are used for clearing airways in patients who have difficulty with secretion management due to neurological conditions, surgery, or respiratory illnesses.
    Portable and stationary suction units are available for rent or purchase.
  13. Diabetes Management Equipment
    Rent/Buy : Devices like glucometers, insulin pumps, and continuous glucose monitoring systems (CGM) are provided for diabetic patients to monitor and manage their condition effectively at home.
  14. Therapeutic Equipment
    Rent/Buy : Equipment like TENS units (Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation) for pain relief or infrared therapy devices for muscle recovery can be rented or purchased for home use.
  15. Pulse Oximeters
    Rent/Buy : Small, portable devices used to monitor oxygen levels in the blood, particularly important for respiratory and heart patients.
    Affordable rental options for short-term use.
  16. Fetal Monitoring Equipment
    Rent/Buy : For expecting mothers, fetal monitors are available to track the baby’s heart rate and overall health.
    Portable fetal dopplers can be rented or purchased for home use.
  17. Rehabilitation Equipment
    Rent/Buy : Equipment like exercise machines, therapy bands, and standing frames are provided for post-surgery or rehabilitation patients.
    Rental options for short-term physiotherapy requirements.
  18. TENS Machines (Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation)
    Rent/Buy : These devices are used for pain relief in conditions like arthritis, post-surgery, or injury recovery.
    Portable TENS units for home care can be rented or bought.
